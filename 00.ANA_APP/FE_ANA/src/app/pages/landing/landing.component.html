Authorisation and Authentication (ANA)

This is a simple Proof Of Concept using DJANGO and DRF as backend, ANGULAR as frontend & TAILWIND for the lock and feel.
Modules
  • Registration.
  • Login and Authentication.
  • JWT based.
  • Two Factor Authenticatiaon with QR Barcode Authenticator.
  • Password Reset with emali token URL.
